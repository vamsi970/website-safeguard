Building a Safer Future: Women’s Empowerment Through Training and Awareness

  • Enhancing Safety for Women Across India
  • Fostering Growth Through Skill Development and Awareness
  • Building a Brighter Future with Equal Opportunities for All

Our Mission

Our SafeGuard application objective is to engage and transform women's lives throughout India through a comprehensive approach of safety and skill-enhancing programs in addition to personal development. Through our safety awareness programs, we enable the woman to become equipped with knowledge that would empower her to protect herself in various circumstances. Our skills training enhances women's capabilities in technology, entrepreneurship, and other vocational fields so as to make them self-reliant. Personal development has been affected in terms of boosting the individual's confidence and developing leadership. Eventually, growth in both personal and professional spheres is realized. Our main objective is to ensure that women succeed or thrive regardless of their backgrounds.

Self-Defense Classes

At Safeguard, we believe that physical safety is a fundamental right. Our self-defense classes teach practical, easy-to-learn techniques drawn from Krav Maga, basic martial arts, and real-life scenarios. These classes are designed to help you protect yourself during potentially dangerous situations, offering you both the skills and confidence to respond effectively. Whether you’re commuting or walking in public spaces, you’ll learn how to defend yourself, de-escalate confrontations, and stay safe.

How it works:

Register through our website to locate a training center near you. Attend free sessions where professional instructors will guide you through self-defense techniques suitable for all ages and fitness levels.

Street Safety & Situational Awareness

Public spaces can be less than safe most of the time, but with the right awareness and knowledge, a lot of risks can be reduced. Our Street Safety & Situational Awareness program deals with the formation of skills to identify possible dangers as well as being aware of their surroundings. Learn how to wander safely around urban environments and choose secure routes in case of emergencies; you will also learn what to do if someone tries to stop or talk to you or gets you into an uncomfortable situation.

How it works:

  • You can register for this course to locate a close-by center that is running the class.
  • Our experts will guide you through how to be situationally aware, avoid bad parts of town, and develop quick thought responses when encountering any dangerous situations.

Online Safety & Cybersecuritys

Keep your virtual life safe, just like any other part of your life, is very important. With emerging cyber threats including identity theft, scams, and cyberbullying, one needs to be more cautious about threats related to the internet. Our Online Safety & Cybersecurity program is designed to teach participants about handling personal data safety, detecting online threats, and online scams.

We are excited to announce the launch of our Online Safety & Cybersecurity program. This initiative will be available through free workshops, allowing participants to engage from the comfort of their homes or at a nearby training center.

Steps to Take:

  • Register for the program here.
  • Engage with us through our sessions on the key issues of password security and safe online practices.
  • Equip and empower yourself with the needed skills to learn how to use the digital world with confidence and security.

Skill Development Programs

Safeguard is committed to safety and personal development. Our skill development programs train them by providing a lot of on-hand training on varied skills. This will range from technology, entrepreneurship, and many creative artistry types. The participant will be equipped with fundamental tools for the appropriate use of digital tools, financial management, and handicrafts. These programs do not only raise individual abilities but also serve as an opportunity for a better personal and professional development, helping the person to become independent in their activities and be more assertive towards their goals, creating a brighter future not just for them but for their communities.

Join us today

  • You can also register through our website free of cost to join some skill development workshops.
  • Acquire knowledge in areas that may help you create business opportunities, enhance employment skills, or satisfy a new personal interest.

Leadership & Empowerment Workshops

There is proper empowerment with self-confidence and reliance. Our Leadership & Empowerment Workshops are linked with building leadership abilities, enhancing communication skills, and emotional intelligence. Overall, these workshops give you the confidence to find your own, develop your individual strengths to create well-being in your personal and professional life. Leaders from all walks of life inspire you to take leadership in your community, at work, and at home.

Training Centers Across India: Empowering Women Everywhere

The Safeguard team is proud of the presentation of 64 dedicated training centers set up across India related to enhancing women's safety and empowerment. The focus areas of these centers are women only, with detailed, comprehensive training and awareness programs designed exclusively to meet the unique challenges that women are faced with today.

Our program would focus on providing an open environment that's free from harm and safely guides women to understand and defend themselves, how to control crises, and be conscious of their safety. Every center has a well-skilled and specialized team in order to provide quality training and assistance to all walks of women.

In regard to critical issues relating to personal safety and well-being, we strive toward the formation of awareness and resilience culture. Attending such programs will not only equip women with practical skills but also with appropriate confidence in navigating situations safely. Our overall goal is the establishment of society that encourages women as active agents for their own safety and supports them in their fight for rights.

The Safeguard team strives to make a real difference in the lives of women all across India, for together, it is possible to make safety and empowerment accessible and possible. Join us on a mission of creating a safer and more inclusive society where every woman can thrive.

From Fear to Freedom: Inspiring Journeys of Transformation

Divya, a 28-year-old from Bengaluru, wanted to start her e-commerce business but had no knowledge of online safety. With the help of the Safeguard app, she learned about the secure payment processing and ways of protecting customer data from cyber threats for business safety.

The 'Sashakti' initiative, a self-defence programme of the Delhi Police, has not only instilled a new sense of confidence in Aarti Chaudhary, a homemaker in Nihal Vihar here, but also given her courage to help other women being harassed on roads.

The Red Brigade Lucknow established by Usha Vishwakarma has empowered thousands of women, mostly survivors of sexual assaults, with martial arts and self-defense training. Since 2011, more than 1.57 lakh have undergone the unique technique called "Nishastra" apart from spreading awareness through street plays and seminars on sexual violence.

Women are respected as goddesses in India; however, despite such religious respect, they face tremendous challenges in daily life. Issues like child marriage at a tender age and illiteracy persist, but the empowerment campaign of women looks for equality and enables them to stand and earn a life independently​.

Pooja Nagpal started teaching self-defense when she was just 15 years old, based on the challenges girls she knew in rural India encountered. She began with "For a Change, Defend," training over 1,000 women and uplifting victims of sex trafficking.
